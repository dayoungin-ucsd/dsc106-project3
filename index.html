Research Question

How have thermal anomaly detections changed across U.S. states from 2020 to 2024?

This project explores geographic patterns of thermal anomalies across the United States and highlights regions with consistently high detection activity over time.

Interactive Visualization

Use the year selector to compare thermal anomaly detections across different years. Darker colors represent higher detection counts. Hover over any state to see its thermal anomaly count, annual average land surface temperature, and vegetation index.
